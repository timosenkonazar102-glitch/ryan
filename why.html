About author

Good afternoon. My name is Nazar and I am 18 years old. My homeland is Ukraine. All my life I live in Bucha city. It is very green and beautiful place. I have graduated from Buchas' Liceum №5. Also I play the bass guitar and have little cover band at home, we usually play classic rock or funk. I am currently studying at Griffith. Here I am studying Computer Science. I chose this field because I have been interested in it since childhood. I like to explore how programs work from the inside. From the discipline, I like WebDev and Java the most. In my free time, I try not to forget to play the bass, and although I don’t have a band here yet, I continue to play solo. This is how I relieve stress after a hard day. And just relax and be alone with myself. I also like to go hiking, especially in the mountains. Fishing is one more hobby.
